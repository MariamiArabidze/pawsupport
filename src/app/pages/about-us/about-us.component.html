About Us

Welcome to Rescue Animal Georgia

At Rescue Animal Georgia our passion is to make a difference in the lives of animals in need. Founded on a deep-rooted commitment to compassion, care, and the well-being of every creature, we have dedicated ourselves to the noble cause of rescuing and safeguarding the most vulnerable members of our planet's diverse and magnificent animal kingdom. Rescue Animal Georgia began as a humble initiative, born out of love for animals and a shared vision of creating a world where every animal can live free from suffering. Over the years, we've grown into a dedicated and dynamic team of animal lovers, united by a common purpose: to rescue, rehabilitate, and provide a second chance for animals in distress.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple but profound: to be a beacon of hope for animals in crisis. We tirelessly work towards:

  • Rescue: We respond swiftly to animals in crisis, whether they are abandoned, abused, injured, or homeless. Our rescue efforts span across species, from domestic pets to wildlife.
  • Rehabilitation: We believe in second chances. We provide medical care, nurturing, and rehabilitation to help each animal recover physically and emotionally, setting them on a path to a brighter future.
  • Education: We understand that change starts with awareness. We strive to educate and inspire communities about the importance of animal welfare and responsible pet ownership.
  • Advocacy: We advocate for policies and practices that protect the rights and well-being of animals. We're a voice for those who cannot speak for themselves.
